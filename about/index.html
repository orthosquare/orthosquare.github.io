About me

I am currently a PhD candidate in the School of Mathematics at Monash University, supervised by Ian Wanless. My areas of interest include Latin Squares, Computational Graph Theory, Theory of Computation, and Algorithm Design. My current areas of study include Perfect 1-factorisations and MOLS of order 10.

While completing a bachelors degree in Computer Science I grew increasingly interested in the mathematical and combinatorial nature of computation.
